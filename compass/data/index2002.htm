Year 2002 Log Data Clustering (k=6)

For Concepts in other topics, they were visited by two patterns. The one is to explore by topics, and the other is to browse one concept in different topics (by concepts). Here, mark these two patterns as Pattern_T and Pattern_C and another mixed one, Pattern_M.

Cluster 1

Size 5
Main path

lever->work_L->force_L<->lever
lever<->ma_L.
Other topics LM, Pulley, Screw, Wedge, WA, IP (well explored)
Pattern Clicks are mostly clustered in lever and IP, distance, force, work and ma were visited many times. Only one general concept (work) was visited. Many topic nodes were visited but not well expored. Pattern_M

Cluster 2

Size 4
Main paths lever<->work_L->energy_L->force_L<->lever
Other topics Three topics, IP, PM, FO were well explored. LM, WA were visited at topic level.
Pattern A few general concept (work, force, energy), and concepts in different topics (work, force) were visited. Task related topics were not well explored. Mainly Pattern_T

 

Cluster 3

Size 6
Main path lever->work_L->force_L<->lever
Other topics WA, CM, LM were visited at topic level. IP, RM, and FO were well explored.
Pattern Some concepts (gravity, friction) in lever were not visited in this cluster, Clicks were centered on force, work, energy, distance, and their corresponding general concepts or other tc nodes were visited. Task related topics were not well explored. Pattern_T

Cluster 6

Size 3
Main path

lever<->work_L-> force_L<->lever
force_L->energy_L->ma_L->force_L->lever
Other topics WA, Pulley and IP were well explored.
Pattern Except the clicks clustered within lever, the task related topics WA and Pulley were explored deeply. Important nodes like force, work, energy, efficiency were browsed, which can help students create global coherence on levers and the related simple machines. Typical Pattern_T. Compared with cluster 5, cluster 6 is another good model to create global coherence.
